Beware of Phishing Attacks:

- Do not give your LDAP USERID & PASSWORD to anyone.
- Do not enter LDAP login information in any pop up widow.
- Do not reply to any mail asking your login information for any
reason.
- Do not enter your LDAP login information on any unknown website.
- Phishing is a fraudulent attempt, usually made through email seeking
your userid, password and personal information.
- If you receive any such email, Please report here immediately.
- Immediately change your passwords if you have accidentally revealed
your credentials.
- Computer Center never asks you for such information.

Example of Phishing Email:

From: support@iitb.ac.in
Sent: Thu, Mar 08, 2012
Subject: UPGRADE EMAIL ACCOUNT
We are currently upgrading our Server for our Account Holders. In order to upgrade your account, you need
to assist us by sending the information below or else your Account might be lost.
Provide the below information :
User Name :
Pass Word :
Regards,
ADMIN, IIT Bombay
